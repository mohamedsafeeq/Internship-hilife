Centered paragraph.

in nature nothing is perfect and everything is perfect...


there is the plesure in the
   pathless woods,
      
    there is a rapture on the 
   lonely share,
   
     there is a socity,where
    none intrudes,
    
      by the deep sea,and music
     and its roar;
     
       I love not man the less,
      But nature more.
